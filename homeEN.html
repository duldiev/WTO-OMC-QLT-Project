ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

On October 18, 2018, during a meeting of the General Council in Geneva, the members of the World Trade Organization unanimously decided to hold the 12th WTO Ministerial Conference (hereinafter - MC-12) on June 8-11, 2020 in the Republic of Kazakhstan, Nur-Sultan.

On October 29, 2019, as part of the visit of the Minister of Trade and Integration of the Republic of Kazakhstan B. Sultanov, an Agreement between Kazakhstan and the WTO on holding the WTO MK-12 was signed in Geneva. Director General of the WTO R. Azevedo stressed that the signing of the Agreement is a clear signal of Kazakhstan's commitment to the principles of the multilateral trading system and WTO rules.

Taking into account the experience in organizing large-scale events, the corresponding status and logistical support, by the order of the Head of State (dated May 21, 2019), JSC NC Qazexpocongress was identified as the main technical contractor and operator of the MK-12 budget. The EXPO Business Center is a cluster with a single architectural solution that combines business, educational, convention and exhibition, as well as entertainment projects in one complex. For the first time during the Ministerial Conferences, the event will be held on the territory of a single complex, where all the conference facilities are within walking distance, as well as a hotel, restaurants and cafes, including the Nur-ALEM Sphere, are located in convenient spaces, which makes it convenient for the MK-12 participants to stay in the EXPO Business Center. 4 locations have been identified for the MC-12 WTO on the territory of the EXPO Business center: the International Exhibition Center, the Congress Center, the Hilton Astana Hotel and the AIFC block C.4.4.
